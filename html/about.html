our story

10 Years of Experience

SiemReap Travel was founded in 2015 by a Cambodian tour guide, who worked for several travel companies himself before and a German traveler. We offer certified tour guides for tours in Angkor Wat and Cambodia. You can choose between English, Spanish or German speaking tour guides. We specialize in private guided tours to the UNESCO world heritage site of Angkor Archaeological Park, commonly referred to as Angkor Wat.

SiemReap Travel is a group of professional and experienced tour guides. We have have been provided tours for many years and have met 1000s of tourists and given them a unique experience of the temples at Angkor Wat.
